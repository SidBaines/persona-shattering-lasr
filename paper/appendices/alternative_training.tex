\section{Alternative training methods explored}\label{sec:appendix-i}

We explored 2 more pipelines for persona training: DPO-only (the first stage of Open Character Training) and SFT-only (the second stage). For both, we use smaller edited answer samples. For SFT-only, we are able to achieve visible behavioural steering on as little as 500 edited samples.
